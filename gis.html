GIS services

  • Zindra Technologies plays a crucial role in providing Geographic Information System (GIS) services to various industries. GIS involves the integration of spatial data with non-spatial data, allowing organizations to analyze, interpret, and visualize information in a geographic context.

    Here are common GIS services offered by Zindra Technologies:

    1. System Design and Integration
    2. Custom Application Development
    3. Data Migration and Conversion
    4. Spatial Analysis and Modeling
    5. Geodatabase Development
    6. Remote Sensing Integration
    7. GPS and Mobile GIS Solutions
    8. Web GIS Development
    9. Training and Support
    10. Integration with IoT (Internet of Things)
    11. Environmental and Urban Planning
    12. Customized GIS Consulting

  • Zindra Technologies brings the industry expertise, and the ability to provide scalable and customized solutions with a strong focus on data security and compliance with relevant standards which is very crucial when dealing with sensitive geographic information.
