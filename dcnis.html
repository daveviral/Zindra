Datacenter And Network Infrastructure

  • Zindra Technologies under its Datacenter And Network Infrastructure portfolio offers a comprehensive suite of solutions to design, deploy, optimize, and manage the IT infrastructure of an organization.

    Here's a summary of the key services provided by us:
    1. Assessment and Planning:
      • Conducting thorough assessments of existing datacenter and network infrastructure.
      • Developing strategic plans aligned with business goals for infrastructure enhancement.
    2. Datacenter Design and Build:
      • Designing and implementing datacenter solutions tailored to specific organizational needs.
      • Managing the physical layout, cooling, power distribution, and hardware deployment.
    3. Network Architecture Design:
      • Designing scalable and secure network architectures to support business operations.
      • Implementing efficient routing, switching, and addressing schemes.
    4. Hyper Converged Infrastructure Solutions:
      • Providing scalable HCI Solution to meet overall Infrastructure needs.
      • Optimizing resource utilization and enhancing flexibility.
      • Building the right backup, DR, and Archival strategy.
      • Helping customers reduce TCO with Cloud to on-premises HCI migrations.
    5. Virtualization, Server and Storage Solutions:
      • Deploying virtualization technologies for servers, storage, and network components.
      • Optimizing resource utilization and enhancing flexibility.
      • Providing scalable server infrastructure to meet computing demands.
      • Implementing storage solutions for efficient data management and retrieval.
    6. Cloud Integration:
      • Integrating on-premises infrastructure with cloud services for hybrid environments.
      • Ensuring seamless data and workload portability between on-premises and cloud platforms.
    7. Network Security:
      • Implementing robust security measures, including firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, and VPNs.
      • Conducting security audits and ensuring compliance with industry regulations.
    8. High Availability and Disaster Recovery:
      • Designing and implementing high-availability solutions to minimize downtime.
      • Developing disaster recovery plans and ensuring data resilience.
    9. Network Monitoring and Management:
      • Deploying monitoring tools to oversee network performance and detect issues proactively.
      • Managing and optimizing network resources for efficiency and reliability.
    10. Wireless Network Solutions:
      • Designing and implementing wireless networks to support mobility and connectivity.
      • Ensuring secure and seamless wireless access across the organization.
    11. Structured Cabling:
      • Designing and installing structured cabling systems for optimal connectivity.
      • Supporting data and voice communication needs with scalable cabling solutions.
    12. Collaboration Solutions:
      • Integrating collaboration tools and unified communication systems.
      • Facilitating efficient communication and collaboration within the organization.
    13. Managed Services:
      • Providing ongoing management, monitoring, and maintenance of datacenter and network infrastructure.
      • Offering help desk support and ensuring system reliability.
    14. Technology Refresh and Upgrades:
      • Planning and executing technology refresh cycles to keep infrastructure current.
      • Upgrading hardware and software components to meet evolving business requirements.
    15. Documentation and Training:
      • Creating comprehensive documentation for infrastructure configurations and processes.
      • Providing training programs for IT staff to effectively manage and operate the infrastructure.
  • With the above services, Zindra Technologies helps organizations achieve a secure, scalable, and efficient IT environment by offering tailored solutions, proactive management, and continuous improvement strategies.
